Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how our Chrome extension "Cefalo Timeout Extension" collects, uses, and protects any information that you give when you use this extension.

Information We Collect

Our extension does not collect any personal data from users. The only data accessed is the content on the Cefalo HR Portal's attendance tab to insert calculated secure end times and display time spent information.

How We Use the Information

The information accessed by our extension is used solely to enhance the attendance report on the Cefalo HR Portal by adding a "Secure End Time" column and displaying the time spent since the first entry.

Data Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. Our extension does not transmit any data to external servers, and all processing is done locally on your device. We have put in place suitable physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we access online.

Cookies

Our extension does not use cookies to store any information.

Third-Party Services

Our extension does not share or sell any user data to third-party services. All data processing is handled locally on the user's device.

User Consent

By using the Cefalo Timeout Extension, you consent to our privacy policy.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes to this privacy policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
